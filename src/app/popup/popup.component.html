Welcome to The Inner Workings of the Cell! In this website, you will learn about the parts of a cell. However, you will need to know a few things before you get started.

On the left side of the page, you can choose whether you want to view an animal cell or a plant cell. Animal cells and plant cells have different organelles.

To select an organelle, click on its button on the left side of the page. When you select an organelle, its description and 3D model will appear in the right side of the page.
